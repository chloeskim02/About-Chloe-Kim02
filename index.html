*About: Chloe Kim*

About Me: I was born in Walnut Creek, California on February 27, 2002. I am a rising junior at Head-Royce School located in Oakland, and I also am a varsity women's coxswain at Oakland Strokes. I live in Lafayette with my mom, dad, and older brother who is a rising senior at the University of Michigan. I also have a 1 year-old corgi named Jetty who I love very much. During the school year I have crew practice for 2.5 hours every week day (4 hours on Saturday and no practice on Sunday). I also take art classes in San Francisco, which I enjoy because it allows me to take time out of my day to relax and I also absolutely love the city.
